Events


Join us for 3 Days of wellness, fun, and outreach by attending our 2023 Festival.

The MIND OF MINE Fesitval dates for 2023 are December 8th-10th. You can choose to attend all 3 days to get the most out of the festival, or purchase a single-day pass to attend one day of the festival. 3-Day Passes and Single-Day Passes are avaliable for purchase through the Festival Store (until sold out!)

This year's events include an array of creative events, wellness classes and activities, and our most popular event, the NAMI Concert Series! Check out the detail event line-up below!

Friday


OUR MINDS:
CREATIVE ARTS
EXHIBITION


Join us on the opening day of the festival for OUR MINDS, an art exhibition featuring and celebrating artists living with mental illness.

View a variety of different works in different mediums, from two dimensional works experiemntal pieces, such as digital media projects and performances.

The opening ceremony is open to all festival attendees, and provides a great opportunity to meet the artists behind the pieces in this year's exhibition. Prints of elligible pieces in the exhibition will be avaliable for purchase directly through the artist, or through the Festival Store, where a percentage of the proceeds from the sales will be dontated to NAMI.
