Events


Join us for 3 Days of wellness, fun, and outreach by attending our 2023 Festival.

The MIND OF MINE Fesitval dates for 2023 are December 8th-10th. You can choose to attend all 3 days to get the most out of the festival, or purchase a single-day pass to attend one day of the festival. 3-Day Passes and Single-Day Passes are avaliable for purchase through the Festival Store (until sold out!)

This year's events include an array of creative events, wellness classes and activities, and our most popular event, the NAMI Concert Series! Check out the detail event line-up below!

Friday


OUR MINDS:
CREATIVE ARTS
EXHIBITION


Join us on the opening day of the festival for OUR MINDS, an art exhibition featuring and celebrating artists living with mental illness.

View a variety of different works in different mediums, from two dimensional works experiemntal pieces, such as digital media projects and performances.

The opening ceremony is open to all festival attendees, and provides a great opportunity to meet the artists behind the pieces in this year's exhibition. Prints of elligible pieces in the exhibition will be avaliable for purchase directly through the artist, or through the Festival Store, where a percentage of the proceeds from the sales will be dontated to NAMI.



Opening Reception: 7pm-9pm
Exhibition Open all 3 days from 11am-10pm

Saturday


MOMENTS OF
MINDFULNESS &
MEDITATION


Are you feeling stressed, anxious, or overwhelmed?

Join us for a relaxing activity session where you can learn about a variety of different mindfulness and meditation techniques from different cultures and regions around the world to help you find what's best for you! Learn the benefits and backgrounds, as well as the science and health response of different techniques, and how to manage and cope stressful situation and circumstances.

This event will be held in an outdoor environment, so make sure to bring a warm jacket just in case. In the case of rain, the event will be moved to an indoor location. Event participants will be notified of any event changes.



Session 1: 9am-11pm
Session 2: 1pm-3pm



RESOURCE FAIR


Our resource fair is an excellent opportunity for you (and your friends and family!) to discover all kinds of different resources, programs, and services available to you and anyone you might know!

Come stop on by, walk past some of the booths, greet some volunteers, make some friends, and grab some freebies! The Resource Fair has a little something to offer for everyone.

Make sure not to miss all the delicious food and drink vendors and trucks while you're walking about too!



12pm-4pm

Sunday


NAMI CONCERT SERIES PRESENTS:

GEORGE EZRA & PHOEBE BRIDGERS


We're sure you won't want to miss MIND OF MINE's highlight event, the NAMI Concert Series and fundraiser! This year's headlining performers are George Ezra (Ambassador for Mind UK) and Phoebe Bridgers. The concert will include a duo set by both Ezra and Bridgers, as well as individual sets by both artists.

Choose how you want to attend: You can attend the concert by purchasing a Single-Day Pass for Sunday, or you can purchase a VIP ticket for preferred box seating near the front of the stage. Can't make it this year? The NAMI Concert Series performance will be available to watch online via livestream!



Doors Open: 8pm
Presented by the National Alliance on Mental Illness
