About


2023 marks the first annual MIND OF MINE festival, a 3-day event weekend helping to raise awareness for the issue that affects 1 in 5 individuals in the US: mental illness.

Partnering with NAMI, the National Alliance on Mental Illness, this festival was created to support those living with mental illness and to raise awareness about and break the stigma around mental health. It can be difficult for many to find the help and resources that they need, especially when in crisis. Our goal and purpose is to help individuals, families, and communities to gain insight into the experiences of those who struggle with mental health.

Presented by the
National Alliance on Mental Illness
